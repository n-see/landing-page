About the Park


Opened in 2023, Super Adventureland has become an iconic family destination. With 16 attractions, there is something to do for everyone in the family. Face each other on Dueling Dragons. Enjoy a river cruise through the jungle on River Adventure. Explore lava fill caverns in Volcanic Escape. Venture under the sea and spot magical aquatic life in our Under the Sea Adventure. No matter your age, you will feel like a kid again.


About Adventure Island


Our 84 acre water park is the home to 20 attractions and pools. Take a tube down Crooked Creek. Or Catch a wave in Otter Pond. There is something for the family to enjoy. All of our pools are kept at 90 degrees to keep the fun going year-round. With our award winning lifeguards from SJC Lifeguard Program, you know safety is our highest priority.

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  • What are the park hours?
      • The park is open daily from 9:00am-10:00pm local standard time.
  • Are there metal detectors?
      • Yes. We ask all guest to pass through metal detectors before entering our parks.
  • Can we bring our own food?
      • No outside food or drinks are allowed in our parks, unless it is for existing medical conditions. Water is aye okay.
  • How much does parking cost?
      • Standard parking rates is $15/day. Parking is free for season pass holders.
  • Is the park seasonal?
      • Our theme park is open year-round, however Adventure Island WaterPark closes in mid-October.
  • How long is my ticket valid?
      • Ticket are valid for 180 days after purchased or once scanned at the turnstile, which ever coems first.
  • Are tickets refundable?
      • Unfortunately tickets are non-refundable or transferable.
  • Are stroller available for rent?
      • Yes, strollers are available for rent for $10/day (while supplies last).
  • What are the height requirements for attractions?
      • Height requirements vary by attractions and are posted at the front of each attraction's entrance.
  • Is the park closed during inclement weather?
      • The theme park doesn't usually close due to inclement weather, however some attractions may temporarily closed. Always check our website for any park closures.
